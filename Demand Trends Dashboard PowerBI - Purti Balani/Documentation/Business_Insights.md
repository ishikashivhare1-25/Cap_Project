# Business Insights & Findings

## Demand Insights
- Ride demand fluctuates significantly over time.
- High-demand periods increase operational pressure.
- Digital payment methods contribute strongly to completed rides.

## Operational Insights
- Higher VTAT negatively impacts completion efficiency.
- Driver-side cancellations contribute to operational leakage.
- Incomplete rides indicate operational failure zones.

## Customer Behavior Insights
- Customer cancellations are concentrated during specific hours.
- Certain pickup locations contribute disproportionately to cancellations.
- Customer satisfaction declines during operational delays.

## Revenue Insights
- Ride monetization varies across demand conditions.
- High-demand periods do not always translate to higher operational efficiency.

## Strategic Recommendations
- Improve driver allocation during peak periods.
- Reduce VTAT to improve completion rates.
- Monitor operational stress continuously.
- Optimize high-risk locations.
